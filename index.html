House Rules · Short Version

Four rules, zero tuition.

We founded this place on December 11, 2024 with one bad name and one good idea. Everything since then has followed the same four rules — the full student handbook spells out the rest.

01

Be kupal, not cruel

Roast your friends all you want. The second it stops being funny for the person on the receiving end, it stops. That's the entire line, and we hold it.

02

Nobody plays alone

Summits, hangouts, obbies, late-night lobbies. If you post "sino laro," someone answers. If you're new, someone drags you into the group photo.

03

Show up as yourself

No auditions, no clout check, no minimum level. Come in your worst avatar and your best mood — or the other way around, we don't mind.

04

Receipts stay public

Five officers run the events, the funds, and the records. Every decision gets written down, checked, and posted where members can see it.

Preamble

Student Discipline

1 statement

Exercising discipline is vital to maintaining peace and stability at Kupal University. This clan highly values courtesy, honesty, and social responsibility. The student council expects students to uphold these values and to conduct themselves in a manner that conforms to this handbook.

Disciplinary Sanctions

Infractions

3 tiers

The penalty for each offense depends on the nature and gravity of the violation. Any of the following, or a combination, may be imposed.

First-time or minor infractions3-day time out

A warning is issued along with a temporary 3-day time out, reminding the student of the relevant rules.

Repeated violations1-week time out

A warning is issued along with a temporary 1-week time out, again citing the relevant rules.

Severe infractionsPermanent

Immediate removal from Kupal University, to safeguard the community's reputation and well-being.

Note

A time out temporarily suspends privileges, including posting and taking part in any discussion.

Disciplinary Cases

Reporting

3 steps

Suspect someone broke a rule? Here's how it gets handled.

  1. Report a suspected violation to the student council through DMs or the designated reporting channel.
  2. Submit authentic evidence to support your claim. False accusations are not tolerated and result in an immediate ban.
  3. Give the council time to investigate and deliberate before deciding what action to take.
